On April 1, 2025 a group of friends and I set sail on Virgin Voyages' Resiliant Lady for a 7-night cruise
through the Caribbean, visiting Colombia and several Caribbean islands. This blog post captures the highlights
of our trip.

The cruise departed from San Juan, Puerto Rico, and it took over 12 hours to get there from San Francisco. We had a brief
layover in Denver, Colorado, before continuing on to San Juan. The flight was long, but we were excited to start our adventure.

<PhotoGallery photos={flightToSanJuan} />

Nick and I arrived in San Juan a day early to explore the city before other friends arrived. We spent the day walking around
Old San Juan, visiting historic sites, and enjoying the local cuisine.
